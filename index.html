About Rhema

Rhema – The Greek word for the "spoken word" of God. Our Bible Quiz Game is designed to make reading the Bible more fun and interactive for people of all ages and backgrounds.

Target: People of all ages and backgrounds interested in exploring the Bible in a way that's engaging, interactive, and fun!

Company Profile: We're passionate about helping people of all ages and backgrounds explore the Bible in a way that's engaging, interactive, and fun! Our App is designed to make Scripture come alive through games, quizzes, and community discussions.

Our Story: Our team of Bible enthusiasts, educators, and tech experts came together with a shared vision: to create a platform that makes Bible study enjoyable and accessible for everyone. We believe that learning about God's Word should be an exciting adventure, not a chore.

Our Mission: Inspire a love for the Bible and its teachings by providing innovative and interactive tools for learning.

Our Values: Faith, Fun, Community, Innovation.
